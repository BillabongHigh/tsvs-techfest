Voice for Peace

Our Contributions

Educating Children in Refugee Camps

We provide structured learning programs, access to educational materials, and trained volunteers to ensure every child continues to learn despite displacement in refugee camps around the world.

Primary Healthcare Initiatives

Mobile clinics and volunteer medical professionals deliver vaccinations, basic treatments, and health education to underserved populations in conflict zones.

Educating Refugees About World Peace

Through workshops, storytelling, and interactive sessions, participants learn conflict resolution skills, the importance of tolerance, and strategies for community building.

Support for Women and Children

We run programs focused on mental health, nutrition, and safe spaces for women and children to recover and thrive even in challenging circumstances.

Distribution of Food and Supplies

Coordinating logistics with local NGOs, we provide timely food and essential supplies to families affected by war.

Promoting Sustainable Community Projects

We empower refugees and host communities with water access, small-scale farming, and local schooling initiatives to help rebuild their lives.

Countries We Have Helped

Syria. 2015. Syrian Civil War. 5 Refugee Camps Set Up

Voice for Peace provided educational programs, food distribution, and primary healthcare to thousands of displaced families in refugee camps across Syria.

Ukraine. 2022. Ukraine War. 4 Refugee Camps Set Up

Our teams delivered medical care, peace workshops, and educational support for children and women affected by the conflict.

Cold War. 1960s-1990s. 3 Refugee Camps Set Up

We provided essential services including schooling, healthcare, and food distribution to displaced families during Cold War-era conflicts.

South Sudan. 2019. South Sudanese Civil War. 4 Refugee Camps Set Up

We supported children and families through education, healthcare services, and peace-building workshops in refugee camps.
